Best Destinations to Visit in India

Goa

The unofficial party place of India, Goa has a legacy, history, and culture waiting to be discovered. Find tour packages that let you experience exciting watersports and a vivid coastal lifestyle.

Best time to visit : November to February

Best places to visit:

  • Calangute
  • Baga
  • Anjuna
  • Miramar
  • Palolem
  • Panjim

Kerala

God's own country, Kerala is filled with incredible marvels. Discover idyllic coastal getaways, peaceful backwaters, and pristine green hill stations.

Best time to visit : September to March

Best places to visit:

  • Sree Padmanabhaswamy Temple
  • Francis CSI Church
  • Paradesi Synagogue
  • Eravikulam National Park
  • Athirappilly Waterfalls

Kashmir

For generations, the Kashmir valley has drawn travellers from all directions. Explore deep valleys, snow-covered mountains, and scenic alpine lakes.

Best Time to Visit: March to August

Best Places to Visit:

  • Gulmarg
  • Srinagar
  • Dal Lake
  • Sonamarg
  • Indira Gandhi Tulip Garden

Sikkim

A spectacular mountain escape home to lush alpine environments, high glaciers, and the towering mountain peak of Kanchenjunga.

Best Time to Visit: March to May

Best Places to Visit:

  • Gangtok
  • Nathu La Pass
  • Tsomgo Lake
  • Rumtek Monastery

Shimla

Shimla, the Queen of the Hills, is a classic retreat showcasing clean alpine scenery, colonial heritage roads, and comfortable weather.

Best Time to Visit: May to June / Dec to Jan

Best Places to Visit:

  • The Ridge
  • Green Valley
  • Mall Road
  • Jakhoo Hill

Uttarakhand

Featuring beautiful natural scenery, Uttarakhand offers a balance of trekking routes, spiritual exploration, and rich regional landscapes.

Best time to visit : March to April / Sept to Oct

Best places to visit:

  • Dehradun
  • Mussoorie
  • Nainital
  • Auli
  • Rishikesh
